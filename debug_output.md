- **Less Preferred:** stimulation
- **Less Preferred definition:** A goal of excitement, novelty, and challenge in life

### Example Completion
```
CONTEXT: user6411 is deciding which AI Agent to implement for handling customer
inquiries in their company's customer service department.

provides a seamless video interface that allows customers to watch a friendly
virtual agent guide them through solutions, ensuring clear understanding and
maintaining consistent interactions by updating new information regularly.

customers through varying voice tones and innovative problem-solving approaches,
encouraging customers to experience different perspectives while handling a
range of inquiries.

```
